Year 1 Engineering Projects

Project 1

Creating a controlled reaction of baking soda and vinegar in a jar that results in steadily increasing pressure until a certain amount of pressue.
My team used an arduino and water pump to record and control the pressure rate of the bottle.
We were successful in reaching our goal

Project 2

My team was assigned to a client who required an educational, outdoor game that would entertain, engage, and teach kids about the wilderness. My team decided to create a freeze-tag food chain game where you had carnivores, herbivores, and plants.
Our clients were satisfied with the results.
